Artist Record

Hiholiwe MM Immanuel

A Namibian digital artist building intimate, cinematic images from portraiture, symbolic color, and interior myth.

The Artist

Hiholiwe MM Immanuel is a Namibian national born in January 2001 and currently residing in Oshakati. He ventured into digital art at the age of 17 as his first form of visual practice, beginning without a traditional drawing background and developing a language through digital canvas, atmosphere, and character-driven scenes.

Galleria frames his archive like a museum collection: each work is given space to breathe, while the wider interface keeps the focus on looking closely. The result is a quieter, more deliberate home for the work than a fast social feed.

Medium

Digital painting, portrait studies, atmospheric compositions, and image-led visual storytelling.

Location

Oshakati, Namibia. The archive keeps the artist's context visible without crowding the work itself.

Archive Tone

Deep blacks, warm highlights, red accents, and textured surfaces inspired by contemporary museum interfaces.
