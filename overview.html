A focused museum shell for an evolving artist archive

Galleria was designed and developed by Abisai "Sai" Sem to showcase artists' work on a digital platform beyond social networking applications. This edition features Hiholiwe MM Immanuel, who agreed to have his likeness and artwork presented in this archive.

The platform can expand into a wider home for visual art: photography, short films, animation, and multiple artist profiles. The updated structure already treats the gallery as a searchable collection, making future categories and artist records easier to introduce.

The artwork and website are protected under intellectual property rights. Please do not duplicate, mimic, repost, or use the work elsewhere without explicit permission.
